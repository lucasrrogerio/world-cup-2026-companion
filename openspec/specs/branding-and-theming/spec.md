# Branding and Theming

Purpose: Definir e aplicar a identidade visual do aplicativo de forma consistente em todos os componentes, garantindo adesão à paleta oficial da Copa 2026.

## Requirements

### Requirement: World Cup 2026 Color Palette
The dark mode theme MUST adhere to a 3-color palette inspired by the World Cup 2026 album identity.

#### Scenario: Dark mode colors
- **WHEN** the user is in dark mode
- **THEN** the primary accent MUST be WC Lime (`#B4FF00`)
- **AND** the secondary color MUST be Vibrant FIFA Blue (`#0076CE`)
- **AND** the background MUST be Ultra Deep Navy (`#00051A`)
- **AND** no more than 3 main brand colors should be used simultaneously to avoid visual clutter.
