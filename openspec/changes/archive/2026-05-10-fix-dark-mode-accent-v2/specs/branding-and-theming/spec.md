# Branding and Theming (Delta)

## Requirements

### Modified Requirements

- **Brand Colors**: The dark mode theme MUST adhere to a 3-color palette inspired by the World Cup 2026 album identity.
- **Primary Accent**: The accent color MUST be WC Lime (`#B4FF00`).
- **Secondary Color**: The secondary color MUST be Vibrant FIFA Blue (`#0076CE`).
- **Background**: The background MUST be Ultra Deep Navy (`#00051A`).
- **Visual Clarity**: No more than 3 main brand colors should be used simultaneously to avoid visual clutter.
